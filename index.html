Objective

The key to securing increased firefighter safety lies in understanding the fundamentals of physics. Legacy training packages continue to focus their materials on viewing fire behaviour from the perspective of chemistry. An alternative branch of scientific enquiry is required.....Physics. This does not demand that we understand Einstein's theories of Special or General Relativity. Fortuntately we need only consider one concept.....Pressure. This website aims to help firefighters educate themselves on the subject of fire behaviour by outlining the key fundamentals to pressure.
